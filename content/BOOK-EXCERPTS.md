## 4. Full-scenario excerpt (~400 words — for "Look Inside" preview on KDP, book sample)

### From Chapter 5: Ego Death

**Description:** You feel your sense of self dissolve completely. There is no "you" — only pure awareness or a merging with everything. It might feel like floating in empty space or falling into a void. Time and identity blur. This can be terrifying or profoundly peaceful, as if you are "dying" and being reborn at the same time.

**Cause:** Psilocybin powerfully quiets the brain's default mode network (the ego center), removing your usual sense of self. This reveals unity with the universe and can feel like your identity dissolves.

**Navigation:**

*Surrender:* gently say, "I release my sense of self," and exhale. Trust that this experience is temporary.

*Affirm return:* silently or aloud remind yourself, "I will return safely." You can prepare a note saying this, and your guide can reassure you out loud.

*Ground yourself:* focus on your breathing or the feeling of your body against a surface. Notice a grounding object (like a stone) or the mattress to stay connected.

*Observe:* try to watch what happens without judgment. Consider thoughts or visions as passing clouds in your awareness.

**Lesson:** In ego death you realize that your true self is boundless and connected to all life. It may be frightening at first, but it often brings deep compassion and insight. A supportive guide (through a hand squeeze or soft words) can help ensure you come back gently to yourself.

**Example:** Maria felt herself melt into "empty space" and panicked. She recalled her note, "I will return." Her guide squeezed her hand and whispered, "Breathe." Maria focused on her breathing and pictured herself floating back like a wave to the shore. Gradually her fear subsided and she felt enveloped in warm light. Afterward she described a profound sense of unity with her family and community.

— *Ego Death is one of 40 scenarios in the* Psilocybin Integration Guide *by Maya Allan. Available on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Waterstones, and more.*
